Privacy Policy

Effective June 13, 2026

Scrye (“Scrye,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with this website (the “Site”). The Site is operated by Scrye LLC.

The short version

We built this Site to collect as little information as possible. It uses no cookies, no analytics, no advertising trackers, and no third-party scripts. We do not track you across websites or build profiles about you.

Information we collect

  • Contact form. The contact form composes a message in your own email application; it does not send data to our servers. We receive only the information you choose to include in the email you send us.
  • Information you send us. If you email us, we receive your name, email address, and the contents of your message, and we retain them to respond and for our records.
  • Server logs. Our hosting provider may automatically record standard technical information — such as IP address, browser type, pages requested, and timestamps — for security, diagnostics, and operation of the Site. This is standard for virtually all websites.

How we use information

We use information to respond to your inquiries; to operate, secure, maintain, and improve the Site; and to comply with legal obligations.

How we share information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Site (for example, hosting and email providers), or where required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Scrye or others.

Data retention

We retain inquiry communications only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by applicable law, and then delete or anonymize them.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live — for example, California (CCPA/CPRA) or the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom (GDPR) — you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of it. We do not sell or share personal information. To exercise any right, email info@scryeinsights.com; we will respond consistent with applicable law and will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Because the Site performs no tracking, it does not respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals.

International users

The Site is operated from the United States. If you access it from outside the United States, you understand that information may be processed in the United States.

Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Export control

The Site contains only general, publicly releasable information. Please do not send us controlled technical data through the Site or by email. Certain technologies may be subject to U.S. export control laws and regulations (ITAR / EAR).

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Effective” date above indicates the latest revision. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
