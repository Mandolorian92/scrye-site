Accessibility Statement

Effective June 13, 2026

Scrye is committed to making this website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We view accessibility as part of building tools for people who operate under real-world constraints.

Conformance target

We aim to conform to the World Wide Web Consortium's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

Measures we take

  • Semantic HTML structure, landmark regions, and a logical heading order.
  • Full keyboard navigability with a visible focus indicator and a “skip to content” link.
  • Text and interface colors chosen to meet WCAG AA contrast ratios.
  • Respect for the operating-system “reduced motion” preference; animation is minimized or disabled accordingly.
  • Text alternatives for meaningful images; purely decorative graphics are hidden from assistive technologies.
  • Clearly labeled form controls and status messages announced to assistive technology.
  • A responsive layout that supports text resizing and zoom without loss of content or function.

Known limitations

We are continually improving. Despite our efforts, some content may not yet fully conform to WCAG 2.1 AA. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know — we treat accessibility issues as defects to be fixed.

Feedback and contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of this Site, please email info@scryeinsights.com with “Accessibility” in the subject line. Please describe the issue, the page involved, and the browser or assistive technology you were using. We aim to acknowledge feedback within five business days and to resolve issues as promptly as we reasonably can.

Compatibility

This Site is designed to work with current versions of major web browsers and assistive technologies. It may not be fully compatible with outdated browsers or software.
