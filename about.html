About

Built by people who've
lived the problem.

Decision-grade geospatial intelligence.

We are a small, focused team building decision-grade geospatial tools for everyone who has to make a call from imagery — whether they're at the tactical edge or behind an enterprise desk.

Our work sits at the intersection of geospatial analysis, intelligence tradecraft, and disciplined software delivery. The people behind it have planned and run operations in the field, built and shipped mission systems, and felt first-hand the distance between the data that happens to be available and the decision that has to be made anyway.

Most tools assume clean data, a reliable network, and time to think. The hardest moments offer none of the three. We build for those moments instead — and we treat honesty about uncertainty as a feature, not a footnote.

The same engine that supports a team in a contested environment helps an insurer judge a property or an investor read a mine site — rapid change detection and confidence-graded intelligence, wherever the decision is made. We work deliberately with a select group of partners across defense and commercial markets, in compliance with applicable law and export-control regulations.

Amando Acosta
